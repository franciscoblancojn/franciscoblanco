Key Projects

Fenextjs
  • An innovative library designed to optimize and simplify development with Next.js. Our mission is to save time and effort on your projects while maintaining quality and scalability.
  • Since 2020, I have been developing Fenextjs. During my experience working on Next.js-based projects, I identified the opportunity to standardize numerous processes, functions, and components. This led me to create a library that includes essential tools to streamline web development.
    Fenextjs provides a wide range of Components, Hooks, Functions, and Interfaces, among other key resources.
  • Some of the most prominent and widely used features in Fenextjs include:
    • Button
    • InputText
    • InputSelect
    • useData
    • useAction
    • useLocalStorage
  • The complete documentation for Fenextjs is available at: https://fenextjs-doc.vercel.app/
Xell
  • CRM, Sales, Warehouses, Products, POS, Cash Registers... all in one platform. Xell combines simplicity, intuitiveness, and robustness in an all-in-one business solution.
  • Xell is a platform I developed over 3 years, designed to efficiently and clearly manage businesses. This solution enables the management of:
    Products, Warehouses, Purchases, Sales, Clients, Suppliers, and Users with different roles.
    I took care of developing an intuitive, fast, and user-friendly interface, designed to validate roles and permissions while maintaining a seamless navigation experience, eliminating the need to reload pages and optimizing workflow.
    Additionally, I designed and developed various libraries for Xell, allowing this large project to be modularized into reusable components and services, which accelerated its development and improved scalability.
  • Some of the key modules and services I developed include:
    • xell-adapters: Versatile adapters to connect multiple services.
    • xell-fenextjs: Advanced tools based on Fenextjs.
    • xell-firebase: Integration with Firebase for storage and database.
    • xell-languages: Multilanguage support for a global experience.
    • xell-svg: Optimized library for handling SVG graphics.
    • xell-email-template: Dynamic and customizable email templates.
